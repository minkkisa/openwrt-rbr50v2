=== LED ANALYSIS ===
V2 GPIO outputs at 10mA (typical LED drive current):
  gpio20: out 1 10mA — LED ON
  gpio21: out 1 10mA — LED ON
  gpio24: out 0 10mA — LED OFF
  gpio25: out 0 10mA — LED OFF
  gpio26: out 0 10mA — LED OFF
  gpio27: out 0 10mA — LED OFF

V2 GPIO outputs at 4mA (could be LEDs):
  gpio22: out 0 4mA — LED OFF
  gpio23: out 0 4mA — LED OFF

GPIO 53-69 are ALL inputs = QPIC NAND bus (confirmed!)
No I2C LED controller (TLC59208F) — GPIO 58,59 are NAND data lines

=== COMPARISON WITH RBR20 (NAND Orbi) ===
RBR20 LED GPIOs:    22, 23, 24, 25, 26, 27
V2 output GPIOs:    20, 21, 22, 23, 24, 25, 26, 27

V2 matches RBR20 LED pattern almost exactly!
Extra GPIOs 20, 21 could be additional LEDs or I2C for backlight LED controller.

=== KEY FINDINGS ===
1. NAND confirmed: GPIO 53-69 all used for QPIC bus
2. LED GPIOs: 20-27 range (matches RBR20 pattern)
3. No I2C LED controller (V1 used TLC59208F on GPIO 58,59 — these are now NAND)
4. UART confirmed: GPIO 16 (TX), GPIO 17 (RX)
5. Reset button: GPIO 18
6. WPS button: GPIO 49
7. PCIe: PERST GPIO 38, wake GPIO 50
8. MDIO: GPIO 6, MDC: GPIO 7
